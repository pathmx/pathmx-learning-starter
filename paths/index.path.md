## How This Works

Everything here runs on one loop: assess where you are, aim at a measurable
target, work through a lesson at your proximal edge, check the evidence, and
record what changed. Paths hold the big loop; each lesson runs a small one.

Your agent maintains this space with you. The files are yours.
