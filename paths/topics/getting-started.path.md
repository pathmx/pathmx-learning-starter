# Getting Started

Turn “teach me anything” into one useful learning episode.

---

## Choose A Goal

Complete this sentence:

> By the end of this session, I want to be able to ________.

A strong goal names something observable: explain a concept, solve a problem,
make a prediction, build a small artifact, or teach the idea back.

---

## Find The Starting Point

Before reading an explanation:

1. Write what you already believe about the topic.
2. Try one small example or prediction.
3. Mark what feels certain, uncertain, or unfamiliar.

This becomes the tutor's initial evidence.

---

## Make The First Learning Move

Choose the smallest useful next move:

- explain one missing idea;
- inspect a worked example;
- correct a misconception;
- practice with feedback;
- advance to a harder transfer task.

Use the [sandbox](../labs/sandbox.lab.md) when the right interaction is still
unclear.

---

## Leave A Next Step

End the session with a durable artifact and one explicit next move. Update the
[learner profile](../profile/index.persona.md) only with information the learner
has confirmed.
