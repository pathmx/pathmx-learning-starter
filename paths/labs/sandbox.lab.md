topic.

## Question

---

## Experiment

Describe one explanation, example, exercise, component, or feedback approach.
Keep the experiment small enough to try once.

---

## Evidence

- What did the learner do?
- What became clearer or more confusing?

## Disposition

Choose one:

- promote into a maintained topic;
- revise and try again;
- preserve as a useful failed experiment;
